Nuti

This site specializes in selling almost all types of nuts. This is
a young team that has recently entered the market and must
stand out from the competition.

Project Overview

CHALLENGE

Nuti is a small company that specializes in selling nuts. They currently solely rely
on social platforms such as Facebook, and Instagram, however, they have the
potential to build their online presence and grow their business with a new
website.

SOLUTION

  • Develop a website that will be able to create your own sets from a selection
    of products
  • Develop the unique style that will stand out among other

ROLE

UX, UI, Front-end

TIME

4 weeks

TOOLS

Figma, Photoshop, VS Code

YEAR

2020

Research

To create a product that will distinguish a young company from competitors, I had to
do research and delve into this topic

Market Research

My start was this market research to get a better idea of online shopping. Marketing research has
helped me gain a deeper understanding of what the market trends are and who the market is made of.
Here are some key findings from my research

  • According to the research, the e-commerce market in 2020 turned out to be one of the most dynamic. It reached $ 4 billion, accounting for 8.8% of total retail sales. At the same time, over the past 5 years, the
    market has almost tripled. Over the next five years, a 2-fold growth is projected.
  • The volume of e-exports in 2020 amounted to about $ 450 million.
  • The most popular monetization model is sales commission.
  • In 2019, e-commerce sales accounted for 14.1% of all retail sales worldwide
  • In 2018, 1.8 billion people purchased goods online. In 2019, 1.92 billion. The trend for digital purchases is continuing to grow.

Competitive analysis

Having studied the trading market as much as possible, I moved on to the analysis of
competitors. Namely, their strengths and weaknesses

User Interviews

At this stage, an interview was conducted with 3 participants who, during the entire
process, answered the questions posed.

  • What do you prefer a way to purchase?
  • Have you ordered nuts online?
  • Do you think it right to give a set of nuts as a gift?
  • Tell us about your first online purchase
  • What can influence your choice?

After the whole interview, I began to delve deeper to understand all the subtleties of
this area.
