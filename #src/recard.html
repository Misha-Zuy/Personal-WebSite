ReCard

This application was created to create an individual design of
bank cards. It will help to distinguish you among faded and not
bright bank cards.

Project Overview

CHALLENGE

ReCard is probably a unique application of its kind that specializes in the design of
bank cards. Many people are tired of gloomy and faded cards and many want to
freshen up their payment method a little.

SOLUTION

  • Develop an application that will help in the creation of the bank card
    design
  • Develop a unique interface that will make this application stand out among
    the few

ROLE

UX, UI

TIME

4 weeks
Self-started project

TOOLS

Figma, Photoshop

YEAR

2020

User Interviews

This stage helped to learn about the frequency of using bank cards, similar experience of using such applications, and more. 4 participants were interviewed

  • How often do you use your bank card?
  • Does the appearance of an element or a device matter to you?
  • In your opinion, can a custom card design make you stand out from the rest?
  • What is the main criterion for you when creating a bank card?
  • Tell me about your experience with similar apps

After interviewing the participants, I analyzed all the answers received and made
conclusions that were reflected in the application.
