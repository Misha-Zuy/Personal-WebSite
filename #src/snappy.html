Snappy

This application was design for delivery in city Sumy. The
main task was to do an accessible and understanding
interface. But also that it to stand out among the
applications.

Project Overview

CHALLENGE

Today, people want to be aware of their orders throughout the entire order and
delivery cycle. Knowing when their order arrived in the kitchen, when their food
was prepared, shipped, and the estimated delivery time is proving to be critical for
today's customers. Therefore, the main focus should be on tracking the courier and
the fulfillment status of the customer order.

SOLUTION

  • Develop an application that will track the whereabouts of the courier and display the status of the order
  • Develop a unique shell for the application in the form of a corporate identity

ROLE

UX, UI Designer

TIME

2.5 weeks

TOOLS

Figma, Photoshop

YEAR

2020

Research Goals

Before starting my research on a project, the first thing I did was set some goals and
create a research plan that would guide my research process.

  • Understand the market trends of the food industry
  • Identify Snappy target market
  • Identify Snappy competitors and assess strengths and weaknesses
  • Understanding what the experience of people who use this application is

Competitive analysis

After researching the market, I started analyzing competitors to find out their strengths
and weaknesses for application in Snappy

User Interviews

At this stage, an interview was conducted with 4 participants, asking them questions
that made me understand their experience with applications

  • How often do you order pizza or sushi?
  • Why are you ordering food in apps?
  • What influences your decisions when choosing a food delivery app?
  • What is the most important aspect for you when using the application?
  • Tell me about your in-app food ordering experience

After the interview, I spent some time getting acquainted with the information received
in order to better understand it.

Main pages of the application

Home

This screen is more important than all the others. Because it contains the important process of this application. This is a choice of the place and time of delivery. It was decided to accommodation it in the tab "Home"

Your poducts

This tab contains the products that you add. Here also you might increase the number and see a total of the selected products. If you pull to the left any product, that you will have the opportunity to delete it

In progress

This tab is the result of successfully completing the previous screens. Here you can track the status of your order and also monitor the courier in real-time. You also can delete the order if you pull to the left
